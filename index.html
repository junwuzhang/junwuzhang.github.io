Hi, I'm Junwu and welcome to my page! I am currently a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, where I am working with Prof. Jeannette Bohg in the Interactive Perception and Robot Learning Lab (IPRL).

Through research in robot learning, human-robot interaction (HRI) and robotic manipulation, I want to design robots that help people. I am especially intrigued to study how robots can:

  1. perceive, model and predict the environment, humans and tasks;
  2. learn from interactions with humans and understand their assistive/collaborative roles;
  3. execute higher-level motion planning and/or lower-level manipulation actions accordingly.
I am passionate about applications in assistive robotics (for older adults or people with physical challenges), shared autonomy, healthcare robotics, industrial automation, human-centered autonomous vehicles, or human-centered AI in general.

I am also on my journey to become a full-stack roboticist! (software, mechanical & electrical).
