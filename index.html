Hi, I'm Junwu and welcome to my page! I am currently a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, where I am working with Prof. Monroe Kennedy in the Assistive Robotics and Manipulation (ARM) Lab.

I am interested in robot learning for human-robot interaction (HRI). I am especially intrigued to study how robots can:

  1. perceive, model and predict the environment, humans and tasks;
  2. learn from interactions with humans and understand their assistive/collaborative roles;
  3. perform higher-level motion planning and/or lower-level manipulation accordingly.
I am passionate about related applications such as assistive robotics (for the elderly or disabled), physical HRI, shared autonomy between human and robots, human-centered autonomous vehicles, or human-centered AI in general.

I am also on my journey to become a full-stack roboticist! (software, mechanical & electrical).
